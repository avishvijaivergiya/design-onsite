About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified family physician with over 15 years of experience in providing comprehensive medical care to patients of all ages. She completed her medical degree at Stanford University School of Medicine and her residency at UCLA Medical Center.

Dr. Johnson is passionate about preventive care and building long-term relationships with her patients. She takes a holistic approach to healthcare, considering both physical and mental well-being in her treatment plans.
